Our Services

WEDDINGS

Get ready to look gorgeous on your special day! Whether you want a soft natural makeup or more accented one, we will find the best solution that suits you and also will look good in the pictures.

FASHION AND MAGAZINES

Makeup looks different in real life than it does on photo and video. That’s why everyday makeup and makeup for the camera can differ. We’ll choose a perfect foundation color to match the skin tone and select the right colors to make your features pop!

VIDEO AND FILM SHOOTINGS

For any type of video shootings (advertisement, video clips, lifestyle videos), makeup services are necessary, whether you need just to enhance skin tone or make a full look. It makes the quality of the final product higher!

BEAUTY AND CREATIVE MAKEUP

Creativity is an important component of makeup! Bright and colored, monochromic, graphic… It’s more than just makeup. It’s art!
